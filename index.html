About Basement

Android App designed to create an enjoyable social platform
in the world of the passionate Home Brewer. Get to know fellow brewers and expand your interest in brewing approaches and recipes.

A community for people like you

When life gets busy, the first thing to go is your passion projects. With Basement we wanted to create a social platform that kept the user constantly inspired and curioius about the wonderous world of home brewing.
